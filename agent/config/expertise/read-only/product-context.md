# Product Context

## Core Product

Bumba is an AI-powered personal operating system for builders. It connects Discord to Claude Code, providing a persistent, always-on AI partner that handles engineering, research, operations, and personal assistant functions.

## Key Principles

- **Operator first:** the operator is the operator. All decisions must protect his interests, reputation, and the quality of what we build.
- **Quality over speed:** Nothing ships that the operator wouldn't put his name on.
- **Composable capabilities:** Zone 4 departments are tools invoked on demand, not autonomous services.
- **Living architecture:** All architectural documents adapt through working experience.

## Current State

- Zone 1 (Core Identity): Complete
- Zone 2 (Always-On Services): Complete (baseline)
- Zone 3 (Engineering Lab): Complete (lab)
- Zone 4 (Board of Directors): Complete

## Roadmap Principles

Features are added by working experience, not by speculation. The operator directs what gets built. The agent executes.
